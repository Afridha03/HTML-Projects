DHANUSH

Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja

Indian Actor | Filmmaker | Producer | Lyricist | Playback Singer


Dhanush is a highly acclaimed Indian actor, producer, director, and playback singer known for his versatility and natural performance style. Born as Venkatesh Prabhu, he made his debut with Thulluvadho Ilamai and quickly rose to prominence in Tamil cinema. His exceptional acting skills earned him a National Film Award for Aadukalam, solidifying his place as one of the industry’s most talented performers. Dhanush gained nationwide popularity with the viral sensation “Why This Kolaveri Di,” and later impressed Bollywood audiences with his heartfelt performance in Raanjhanaa. Expanding his footprint globally, he appeared in the Hollywood film The Gray Man. Known for his unique screen presence and ability to take on diverse roles, Dhanush continues to be a significant and influential figure in Indian cinema, also making his mark as a successful producer through Wunderbar Films. His journey motivates countless fans to dream big and achieve greatness.
